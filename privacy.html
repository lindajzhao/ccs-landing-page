Privacy Policy

Childhood Cancer Survivor Canada / Survivant d’un cancer pédiatrique Canada

Collection, Use, Disclosure, and Retention of Personal Information

Childhood Cancer Survivor Canada (CCSC)/Survivant d’un cancer pédiatrique Canada (SCPC) is committed to protecting your privacy.

We only collect personal information about you which you provide to us. The purpose for which personal information is collected will be disclosed to you prior to its collection. Personal information may includes your name, date of birth, mailing address, and/or email address.

We use personal information in accordance with the purposes for which it is collected, which may include processing donations, sending you information you request or consent to receiving, and responding to your questions or concerns.

We do not sell or disclose your personal information to third parties. We retain your personal information to the extent we need it to fulfil the purposes for which it is collected.

Safeguarding Personal Information

We take industry standard steps to safeguard your personal information against loss, destruction, alteration, unauthorized access, or unlawful disclosure.

Web Browser Privacy

Our web server automatically creates and maintains logs of standard information obtained from your browser including your IP address and pages requested. This information is collected for statistical purposes to allow us to deliver better content.

Access to Personal Information & Consent

You may request access and/or correction to your personal information that CCSC/SCPC has in its control. You may withdraw your consent at any time to the collection, use or disclosure of your personal information.

Contact Information

For any inquiries about your personal information, please contact legal@childhoodcancersurvivor.org
